Speech-to-Text is an online tool that helps you transcribe your audio file and generate a summary of the text. Just follow these simple rules before getting started:

  1. Your file must be of the extension .flac,
  2. The audio clip must be recorded in 44100Hz.
  3. Make sure your audio is either a formal speech, an expository/argumentative essay, a news recording, or basically anything that has a clear diction and no dialogues!
